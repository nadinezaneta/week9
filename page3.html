Best Ramen in Toronto

Sansotei is a Canadian brand specializing in authentic Tonkotsu Ramen taking inspiration from the Kyushu and Hokkaido regions of Japan. They source the highest quality products and ingredients both locally and direct from Japanese suppliers. Sansotei strive to build a culture of hard work and dedication, and they are passionate about serving the best ramen, one bowl at a time. You should try Sansotei ramen sometime soon!
